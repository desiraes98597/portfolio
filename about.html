Hello!

My name is Denise

I spent time from 2008-2010 going to school and getting a web design certificate.

Graduated with honors, but got cold feet.

Everyone around me was young and seemed so much better than me. I suffered from imposter syndrome, big time. So instead of looking for working in the field I passionately loved, I took some time to recharge and ended up living and working in Yellowstone for 3 years. Coming home to Seattle, I found a job at Amazon, and basically did not code again until 6 months ago.

I consider myself very lucky to be in a role at work where I have the flexibility to try new things. I started coding again and remember how much I loved it. So here I am, trying to update some very outdated skills, and learn javascript, which in 2010 was not really taught for web design.

I did keep my portfolio on line even though I have never update it. If you have any interest in checking it out.
